Frequently Asked Questions

What is RE Intelligence?

RE Intelligence is a real estate value and rent milestone dashboard covering 210+ districts across major global cities. It helps you find where opportunity meets quality of life, backed by real data.

Is the app free?

Yes. RE Intelligence is completely free to download and use.

What data does the app collect?

RE Intelligence does not collect any personal data. Please see our Privacy Policy for full details.

Which cities are covered?

The app covers major global cities with data across 210+ districts, and we are continuously expanding coverage.

How do I report a problem?

Send an email to t-ii@windowslive.com with your device model, iOS/Android version, and a description of the problem. We will get back to you as soon as possible.
