RE Intelligence - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Introduction

RE Intelligence is a real estate analytics application that provides district-level scoring and insights across Saudi Arabian cities. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

RE Intelligence does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device and does not require user accounts, login credentials, or any form of registration.

Data Storage

All data displayed in the app is bundled within the application itself. No data is sent to or received from external servers.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Apple Maps/Google Maps for map display functionality. Please refer to their respective privacy policies.

Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
